Benchmark Description

U233-MET-FAST-001
"233U Jezebel: A Bare Sphere of Uranium-233 Metal"
Part of the International Criticality Safety Benchmark Evaluation Project (ICSBEP)

Main materials: U-233 (metal)
Spectrum: fast
Detailed description: 
- bare sphere of U-233 metal

Criticality (MCNP): k_eff = 0.9970 +/- 0.0011
(2500 neutrons per cycle, 115 cycles, 15 inactive)
