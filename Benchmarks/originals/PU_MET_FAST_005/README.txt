Benchmark Description

PU-MET-FAST-005
"Benchmark Critical Experiment of a Plutonium Sphere Reflected by Tungsten"
Part of the International Criticality Safety Benchmark Evaluation Project (ICSBEP)

Main materials: Pu-239 (metal), W
Spectrum: fast
Detailed description: 
- sphere of Pu-239 metal, surrounded by tungsten reflector

Criticality (MCNP): k_eff = 1.0080 +/- 0.0010
(1000 neutrons per cycle, 310 cycles, 10 inactive)
